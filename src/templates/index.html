HOW LOOKOUT

WORKS?

We start by decomposing video and audio into frames and time-based segments to ensure frame-level and temporal precision. Next, we extract multimodal features — visual patterns, motion dynamics, and audio spectrograms — capturing even subtle traces of manipulation. Finally, our AI models perform cross-modal analysis, combining visual and audio signals to detect inconsistencies and deliver an accurate, confidence-scored result.
